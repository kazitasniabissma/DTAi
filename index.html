The Roadmap

How We Drive Change

August to November 2026 — four months, four pillars, one transformed city.

01
Community Workshops at BRAC University
Four awareness workshops bringing together students, residents, and local authorities to discuss responsible road behaviour and safer mobility. Each session targets 100+ attendees.
02
Outreach to Transport Workers
Distributing 1,000 traffic safety guides and reflective stickers to rickshaw pullers and bus drivers around Mohakhali — supporting those who spend the most time on Dhaka's roads.
03
#DhakaMoves Digital Campaign
A social media campaign targeting 2,000+ engagements through posts, shares, and discussions that promote safer commuting habits and build a community of conscious road users.
04
Impact Measurement & Advocacy
Pre- and post-workshop surveys to measure awareness improvements. Findings presented to local authorities and BRAC University to advocate for long-term structural change.
